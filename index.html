COMMAND HUB

♔ KING (Royal): The absolute core asset. It moves exactly one square in any direction. Its survival is the primary win condition; if the King is trapped, the simulation terminates immediately.
Example: Moving the King to a safe file to escape a long-range Rook suppression.
| Importance: Critical
♕ QUEEN (Royal): The simulation's offensive powerhouse. Capable of moving any distance straight or diagonally, combining the firepower of a Rook and Bishop.
Example: Executing a full-board sweep to deliver a finishing blow from the centre.
| Importance: Critical
♖ ROOK (Heavy): Specialized in linear suppression. Rooks move horizontally or vertically across open ranks and files, and are essential for endgame terminal sequences.
Example: Occupying the 7th rank to restrict enemy King movement.
| Importance: Significant
♗ BISHOP (Heavy): A diagonal specialist. Bishops move any distance diagonally but are confined to tiles of their starting color. They excel in pinning assets from long range.
Example: Pinning an enemy Knight to their King across the central diagonal.
| Importance: Significant
♘ KNIGHT (Minor): Master of asymmetrical tactical depth. Moves in an 'L' shape and is the only asset capable of leaping over obstacles.
Example: Jumping over a pawn wall to fork the enemy King and Queen simultaneously.
| Importance: Moderate
♙ PAWN (Infantry): The soul of the simulation. Pawns move forward but capture diagonally. Reaching the final rank triggers a Promotion Event.
Example: Sacrificing a pawn to open a line for a heavier asset.
| Importance: Expendable
