Team 1 Activation
The Pawn: The fundamental infantry unit. Moves one forward, yet captures diagonally. Upon its initial move, it may advance two squares. Reaching terminal rank allows for promotion.
The Knight: A specialized tactical unit that navigates in a unique 'L' pattern. Maintains the singular ability to leap over intervening pieces.
The Bishop: A sophisticated long-range operative sliding any distance along diagonal trajectories. Bound to its starting colour.
The Rook: Formidable heavy piece moving in straight horizontal or vertical lines. Plays a critical role in king safety through the castling maneuver.
The Queen: Most versatile asset. Combines movement capabilities of both Rook and Bishop.
The King: The pivotal figure around which the entire game revolves. Preservation is absolute.
⭐ Best Move: Signifies the absolute strongest continuation identified by engine analysis.
💀 Total Blunder: Catastrophic error that leads to an immediate loss of the game or major piece.
