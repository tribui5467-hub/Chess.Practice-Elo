MOVEMENT PERMITS

♔
KING // COMMANDER
  • Moves exactly 1 square in any direction (Omni-directional).
  • Restriction: Cannot move into a square controlled by an enemy unit.
  • Permit: Can Castle with a Rook if neither has moved and the path is clear.
♕
QUEEN // OVERSEER
  • Unlimited movement across clear ranks, files, and diagonals.
  • Permit: Combines the movement logic of the Rook and Bishop.
  • Restriction: Cannot leap over other units.
♖
ROOK // BULWARK
  • Unlimited movement horizontally or vertically.
  • Permit: Essential for the Castling maneuver.
♗
BISHOP // INQUISITOR
  • Unlimited movement along diagonal vectors only.
  • Restriction: Bound to the colour of its starting sector (Light/Dark).
♘
KNIGHT // VANGUARD
  • Moves in a fixed "L" shape (2 squares then 1 square perpendicular).
  • Unique Permit: Bypasses all structural blocks (Jumps over units).
♙
PAWN // INFANTRY
  • Moves 1 square forward. Captures 1 square diagonally forward.
  • Permit: Can move 2 squares forward on its initial deployment.
  • Permit: En Passant — can capture a pawn that has just double-stepped past it.
  • Permit: Triggers Promotion upon reaching the final Rank.
