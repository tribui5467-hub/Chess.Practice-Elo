♘
The Knight: Infiltration Specialist. Only unit capable of bypassing structural blocks via non-linear "L" trajectory.
♙
The Pawn: Frontline Infantry. Forward movement only; diagonal capture. Strategic importance for Rank-8 Promotion.

Best Move: The single most efficient engine calculation for the current sector.

Book: Moves verified by deep theoretical historical data (Openings).

Checkmate: Tactical mission failure. The King has zero legal escape vectors.

Stalemate: Gridlock. The player to move is not in check but has zero legal trajectories. Results in a strategic draw.
